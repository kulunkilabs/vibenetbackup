Email Setup Guide
Gmail / Google Workspace

Requires an App Password (2FA must be enabled). Apprise auto-detects Gmail SMTP settings.

mailto://youruser:app-password@gmail.com?to=recipient@example.com
Outlook / Microsoft 365

Use an app password or your regular credentials if SMTP AUTH is enabled by your admin.

mailto://youruser:password@outlook.com?to=recipient@example.com
Company SMTP Relay (no auth, port 25)

Many corporate environments run an internal relay that accepts mail without authentication. Use mailto:// (no encryption) and specify the relay host with smtp=.

mailto://relay.company.local:25?from=vibenetbackup@company.com&to=ops@company.com&smtp=relay.company.local
Company SMTP with TLS (port 587)

Use mailtos:// for STARTTLS on port 587.

mailtos://user:password@smtp.company.com:587?from=backups@company.com&to=ops@company.com
Multiple Recipients
mailto://user:pass@gmail.com?to=admin@co.com,ops@co.com&cc=manager@co.com
